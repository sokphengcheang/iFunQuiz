Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Carapacik Space (“we”, “our”) operates the iFun Quiz mobile application. This page describes how we handle information when you use the app.

Information we collect

iFun Quiz is designed to use minimal data:

  • Data stored on your device only: Your game progress, settings (e.g. theme, language, color mode), statistics (streaks, win rate, guess distribution), and level progress are stored locally on your device. We do not receive or store this data on our servers.
  • No account required: You do not need to create an account or provide your name, email, or other personal information to use the app.

Third-party services

The app may use the following services:

  • Apple App Store: If you download the app from the App Store, Apple’s privacy practices apply to that process. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Sharing and sharing features

When you use the in-app “Share” feature (e.g. to share your result), the content is sent through your device’s standard sharing mechanism (e.g. Messages, Mail, social apps). We do not receive or store what you share or with whom.

We do not sell your data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children

iFun Quiz does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app can be used without providing personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
